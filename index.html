About Help Artist

Help Artist India was founded in January of 2018. It acknowledges and harbor the veiled, unidentified artists, availing nothing but facilitating the unrevealed talents inhabiting our society. This NGO dreams of re- establishing the sphere of talents and proclaiming the worth of any artist who behold the talent and brew it for the audience. Artists can find this organization extremely helpful and facilitating. Above all saving the Heritage of the India, what makes someone inspiring, keeping the zeal alive even when the circumstances are not favorable. F.A.Q

  • What is Help Artist India, and what is its mission?

    Help Artist India is an NGO founded in January 2018 with the mission to support and promote hidden and undiscovered artists in India. We aim to re-establish the sphere of talents and highlight the value of artists, regardless of their circumstances or backgrounds.

  • How can I get involved with Help Artist India as an artist or supporter?

    You can get involved with Help Artist India by participating in talent-based events, joining our fundraising initiatives, or contributing to our campaigns and social work. Artists can also reach out to us for assistance in showcasing their talents.

  • What types of talent-based events does Help Artist India organize?

    Help Artist India organizes various talent-based events, including art exhibitions, music festivals, dance performances, and more. These events provide a platform for artists to showcase their skills and gain recognition.

  • How can I contribute to Help Artist India's fundraising efforts?

    You can contribute to Help Artist India's fundraising efforts by donating directly to our cause, participating in fundraising events, or volunteering your time and skills to support our initiatives. Your contributions help us continue our mission of empowering artists.

  • What is the main focus of Help Artist India's social work and campaigns?

    Help Artist India's social work and campaigns primarily focus on preserving and promoting India's artistic heritage. We also work to inspire and support artists who may face challenges such as health issues, old age, financial constraints, or being outside the mainstream art scene. Our campaigns aim to uplift and celebrate the creativity of every artist.
